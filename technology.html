Launch vehicle

A launch vehicle or carrier rocket is a rocket-propelled vehicle used to carry a payload from Earth's surface to space, usually to Earth orbit or beyond. Our JSF Space Shuttle was a two-stage, fully reusable launch vehicle designed for space shuttle missions mounted on the external tank.
